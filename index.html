I’m Yiannis* Tsiamas, an AI Research Scientist and Ph.D. candidate at UPC Barcelona, working on multilinguality, multimodality, text/speech translation, and representation learning. I have authored papers at top-tier conferences like ACL, EMNLP, and ICASSP, and I’ve had the privilege of contributing to cutting-edge research during internships at Meta, Apple, and Dolby.

I am passionate about languages and my goal is to make AI systems trully multilingual, and accessible to everyone.

* I use Ioannis for publications and formal settings but overall prefer Yiannis, which is the casual version of my first name.

Experience

  1. AI Research Scientist Intern

    Meta FAIR August 2024 – Present
    I work within the Omnilingual Machine Translation team to expland the language coverage of MT systems well beyond their current capacity.

  2. AI Research Scientist Intern

    Apple April 2024 – July 2024
    Contributed to the Machine Translation team within the AI/ML division, primarly focused on Prosody Evaluation and Speech Translation. My research, which was published in WMT 2024, showed that although current state-of-the-art Speech Translation systems do have a understanding of prosody, they rarely leverge it in practice, indicating significant room for improvement.

  3. AI Research Scientist Intern

    Dolby November 2023 – February 2024
    Conducted research on Audio-Visual Representations and Contrastive Learning. Also contributed to a project on Video-to-Audio Generation. The corresponding research papers were published at ICASSP 2025 and ECCV 2024.

  4. AI Research Scientist Intern

    Zeta Alpha November 2019 – June 2020
    This internship was part of my MSc thesis. I worked on passage ranking and answer style transfer for complex question answering using transformer-based models.

  5. Financial Analyst Intern

    Eurobank June 2015 – July 2015
    My work during this internship focused on analyzing financial statements, adjusting funding limits and evaluating business plans of local businesses in Athens.
